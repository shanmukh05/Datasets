# [Go to Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/shanmukh05/music-classification)

## Content

   Dataset consists of 12 folders (12 different moods).
   
   In each folder there are ***.wav*** music files (5sec duration) corresponding to given mood.
    
    aggressive
    dramatic
    eerie
    happy
    mellow
    mysterious
    romantic
    sad
    smooth
    upbeat
    uplifting
    whimsical
